About AC0/RD

AC0/RD is a community of open-source developers creating software & media that innovate on what's available. To do this, we work with many companies and developers around the world, by contributing to the development of their software and participating in their competitions We are motivated by innovative approaches, innovative software and where this will take us, whether it's making our own software or contributing to projects maintained by other entities
