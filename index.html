What we do

ACORD Robotics is a software, scientific engineering and writing organisation that is based and was founded in Australia by Nicholas Antipas (COO) & Liam Arbuckle (Chairman), originally under the name Alliance of Droids. Our goal is to create great content in any medium we choose to develop our skills in and use that content to improve the lives of people around the globe

This website

This website serves both as a means of letting you know more about ACORD, as well as a way for our members to communicate and for you to join our team. There are 2 types of memberships - the community membership & the "paywall" membership. You can sign up for both for free.

Microblog

Our users can post simple, quick messages on the timeline by logging into the members-section of this website. Check out what we're up to!

Download our software

All our games and apps are free and open-source FOREVER. Download them from this website quickly and easily, and have your say on what we add next!
